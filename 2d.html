Charities to support

  • Committee to Protect Journalists
    • The Committee to Protect Journalists is an independent organization that promotes press freedom worldwide.
    • CPJ is made up of about 40 experts around the world, with headquaters in New York City.
    • CPJ's work is based on its research, which provides a global snapshot of obstructions to a free press world. CPJ's research staff documents hundreds of attacks on the press each year.
  • The Southern Poverty Law Center
    • The SPLC is the premiere U.S. non-profit organzation monitoring the activities of domestic hate groups and other extremists.
    • The SPLC's Teaching Tolerance Project combats prejudice among our nation's youth while promoting equality, inclusiveness and equitable learning environments in the classroom.
    • The Bob Woodruff Foundation helps veterns navigate the broad range of non-profits, NGO's and federal agencies to find the most innovative programs to meet veteran needs.
    • While reporting from Iraq in January 2006, Woodruff was seriously injured by an IED that struck his vehicle. This experience inspired Bob, and his wife, Lee, to make sure veterans have access to the highest levels of care and support.
